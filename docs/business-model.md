# Business model — LBR

Independent public-sector market-entry compliance for Liberia.

- Public Procurement and Concessions Commission (PPCC) public
  procurement -- Amendment and Restatement of the Public Procurement
  and Concessions Act, 2010 (approved January 12, 2026; PPCC's own
  site names three confirmed generations of this Act: 2005, 2010, and
  this current 2026 restatement)
- Liberia Business Registry (LBR, an autonomous agency) business/
  company registration under the Associations Law, Title 5 of the
  Liberian Code of Laws Revised -- registration form RF-001, Articles
  of Incorporation, Empowered Person Form (Form A) / Registered Agent
  Form (Form B), name reservation valid 120 days (~USD $15.00)
- Liberia Revenue Authority (LRA) Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN)
  registration -- Liberia Consolidated Revenue Code As Amended - 2020
- Investment Act of 2010 (National Investment Commission) Schedule
  gate -- bars a foreign-company engagement from a Schedule-restricted
  business activity unless its own declared capital clears the
  threshold for its own declared ownership structure (US$500,000
  wholly foreign-owned / US$300,000 in a >=25%-Liberian partnership),
  or bars it categorically if the activity is one of the sixteen
  reserved-for-Liberians activities
